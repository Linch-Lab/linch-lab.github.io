abstract: "This study addresses the critical need to enhance the performance of protonic ceramic fuel cells (PCFCs) by engineering a porous interlayer between the cathode and electrolyte. A porous interlayer composed of the electrolyte material, barium cerium zirconium yttrium oxide electrolyte (BaCe0.6Zr0.2Y0.2O3-delta, BCZY) was prepared at various calcination temperatures to optimize its microstructure. With the optimized calcination temperature of 1350 degrees C, the porous interlayer forms effective contact with the lanthanum strontium cobalt ferrite cathode (La0.8Sr0.2Co0.8Fe0.2O3-delta, LSCF), enhancing the number of triple-phase boundary reaction sites for oxygen reduction. A PCFC with an optimal porous interlayer achieved a peak power density of 517 mW/cm2 at 800 degrees C, representing an 11.6 % improvement over the fuel cell without the porous interlayer. This enhancement is attributed to the extension of electrochemically active areas, which reduces polarization resistance by 23.8 %."

### 研究亮點



* **優化微結構控制**：研究確立了 $1350$ °C 為最佳煅燒溫度，能使 BCZY 多孔中間層與 LSCF 陰極之間形成最有效的物理接觸。
* **擴展反應活性區域**：透過導入多孔中間層，顯著增加了氧還原反應所需的**三相界面（Triple-phase boundary, TPB）**位點。
* **提升功率輸出與效率**：在 $800$ °C 時，峰值功率密度達到 $517$ mW/cm²，較無中間層的電池提升了 $11.6\%$，並成功降低了 $23.8\%$ 的極化電阻。
